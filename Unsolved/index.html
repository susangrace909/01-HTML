Susan Pero

Hello! My name is Susan Pero. I grew up in Orlando, Florida, and have lived here my whole life. I originally studied Music Education and the University of Florida, where I received my Bachelor's and Master's. I taught elementary music for three years before going back to school. I then went to Valencia College where I got my Associate's in Plant Science. From there, I became Head Grower at a local cactus nursery. Currently I am in charge of keeping plants alive from the time we receive them until we sell them. This means my duties range from watering, to scouting for pests, to spraying chemicals. I am choosing to leave the profession because it is a very physical job and I know that it is not sustainable. I would like to transition to a job that will be doable for the rest of my career.
